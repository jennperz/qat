Meet our Team

QAT is comprised of representatives from the CPA, DIR, LBB, and SAO. General inquiries and all submissions should go to the QAT email address at QAT@dir.texas.gov where all members receive a copy of the email.

Heather Hardy

Director, Strategic Sourcing Digital Project Services

Heather has over 20 years of experience in government and information technology with the private sector, federal and Texas state government. Prior to her time at DIR, Heather served as Director, Federal State Coordination at the Texas Health and Human Services Commission for 6 years. Heather is a native Austinite who graduated from the University of Texas at Austin.
